GAME SUMMARY

Ignited Steel it's a rogue-like tactics game with a sci-fi setting. The main idea of the game is the managing of heat. The player controls a squad of old mechs with overheating issues due to the attack of an evil AI wich has render useless current technology.
Overheating the mechs can be as dangerous as can be useful for the player. With more heat all damage both received and dealt is increased, making crucial the correct managing of each mech's heat.

Out of combat, the player will be able to modify each mech weapon, support equipment and core, modifying their attributes and their actions. In their journey, the player will not only fight but also encounter themselves in unexepcted events that will provoque unexepcted consequences in every run
