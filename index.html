About Me

Hello there! My name is Jorge Rodriguez and I'm a Junior Game Designer with one year of experience as an indie developer. I have participated in several projects while i was studying a Game Design Degree and after that, I spent one year developing my own indie game with a group of ex classmates: Ignited Steel.
